Our Mission

Slackwater Supply has served fishers for generations, bringing innovating fishing technology to the recreational fishing community. It is our goal to ensure the community is not only safe while fishing, but also has an enjoyable and productive time. We strive to keep close to time-honored fishing traditions while providing the community with modern technologies.
