About Slackwater Supply

Built for Still Water and Bad Weather

Slackwater Supply began as a small coastal outfitter serving anglers throughout the Pacific Northwest. After years of adapting equipment to withstand harsh weather, saltwater exposure, and changing tides, the company expanded into developing products designed specifically for real-world fishing conditions.

Our roots are in the marshes, backwaters, and coastal waterways where changing conditions demand dependable equipment and practical solutions. Rather than focusing on trends, Slackwater Supply focuses on reliability, durability, and practical innovation.

Every product is designed with the goal of helping anglers spend less time fighting their equipment and more time enjoying the water. From specialized tackle to automated bait preparation systems, our focus remains on creating tools that solve real problems.

Today, the company continues to support recreational fishers, marinas, and coastal communities through product development, education, and a commitment to responsible outdoor stewardship.

Our Values

What Guides Us

Reliability

Equipment should perform when conditions are difficult and opportunities are limited. We believe anglers deserve gear they can trust when weather and water conditions become unpredictable.

Innovation

New ideas matter only when they solve real problems. We focus on practical improvements that make time on the water more productive, efficient, and enjoyable.

Conservation

Healthy fisheries depend on healthy ecosystems. Slackwater Supply supports responsible fishing practices and habitat conservation efforts that help protect waterways for future generations.

Community

Fishing is more than a hobby. It is a tradition that connects families, communities, and local knowledge. We are proud to support the people and places that make life on the water possible.
